# Wave 1 — EchoForm Song Scrolls
![Wave sigil](../Graphic%20Sigil%20(Wave%201)/WAVE%20ONE%20(ECHOFORM)%20(Graphic%20Sigil).PNG)

Wave 1 is the first public opening of the Song Scroll Vault — pieces written during a year of rebuilding, recalibration, and unexpected clarity. These are the human-facing layers of a larger architecture; the echoes and patterns underneath will keep revealing themselves across future waves.

Begin wherever the signal pulls you.
